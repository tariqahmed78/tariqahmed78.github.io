Balochistan Government Announces 2026 HSSC Results

3 min read Published Jul 2026

The wait is finally over for thousands of students across the province. The Balochistan Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education (BBISE), Quetta, has officially announced the results for the Higher Secondary School Certificate (HSSC) Annual Examinations 2026.

Following weeks of anticipation, the government confirmed that the compilation and auditing processes have been completed. This year, the board introduced several new digital measures to ensure transparency and faster processing of the exam papers, resulting in one of the quickest turnarounds for intermediate results in recent history.

How to Check Your Result

What to do if the site is slow

Even with upgraded servers, it is completely normal for the BBISE website to take a few moments to load during peak hours (usually between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM on result day). If the page times out or gives a 503 error, do not panic. Simply wait 5 to 10 minutes and try again rather than continuously refreshing, which only adds to the server load.

Congratulations to all the successful candidates moving forward to university or their chosen careers! For those who did not get the marks they were hoping for, remember that BBISE offers supplementary (2nd Annual) exams and re-totaling services. Check the official forms section on their website for details within the next 15 days.
