Website Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED — JULY 2026

This policy covers trtechmedia.com — the website itself, including its articles and product pages. Looking for the Fiber Router Manager Android app's privacy policy instead? See it here.

01Information we collect

You can read this entire website without providing any personal information. We collect information only in these situations:

  • Like button: when you like an article, we store a one-way hash of your IP address (not the IP itself) to prevent duplicate likes from the same visitor. This cannot be used to identify you.
  • Basic usage analytics: we may use aggregated, anonymous analytics to see which pages are read most. This data does not identify you personally.

02Cookies & advertising

This website may display advertising through Google AdSense. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites across the web.

You can learn more about how Google uses this data, and manage your ad personalization settings, at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

03Third-party links

This site links out to third-party services, including the Google Play Store. We don't control and aren't responsible for the privacy practices of sites you reach through these links.

04Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through it.

05Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "last updated" date at the top of this page.
